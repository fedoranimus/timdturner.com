TempurLab
Environment Data Visualization
Overview

TempurLab is a lab environment monitoring system for the New York University Laboratory for Molecular Nanoscience.

Lab Sensors

There are many sensors laid out in strategic locations around the lab. They detect either temperature or humidity. Each one has a variable accepted standard deviation.

If a sensor is outside of their accepted range, its status icon changes color. This site is available from any device with a data connection and is displayed on a tablet outside of the lab for easy viewing.

Probe Data

Each probe stores data about itself. The last four hours are available by hover. A click or tap on the probe will display a detailed and searchable database of values.

Final Thoughts

Due to short development frames and necessity, the visual design is a bit to be desired. I hope to improve that in the future when the need arises. You can explore the system here.
